Fitaviz visualization of missing values in HR and step minute-level data White regions occupy the time periods occurring before the patients entered the study or after they exited. Stacked bars in Panel A represent the number of minutes per day and their color distinguishes between: minutes with observed steps but missing HR (light gray), minutes with missing steps and observed HR (black), minutes with missing steps and missing HR (dark gray), and minutes with observed HR and observed steps (orange).
