Fitbit intensity levels from a random sample of minutes and their corresponding step and HR combinations Fitbit's proprietary algorithms classify minute activity in four intensity levels: sedentary, light, moderate and active. The stratification in four follows the report on Physical Activity Guidelines for Americans (PAGA) by the US Department of Health and Human Services. Fitbit classification varies among individuals and its intensity levels are personalized. Unfortunately, it is not possible to explain what are the underlying drivers of the classification.
