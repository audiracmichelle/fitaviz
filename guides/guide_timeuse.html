Fitaviz visualization of the daily time-use descriptors split in valid and non-valid days. The proportion of time a participant spends in each activity level per day.

Ideally the decisions regarding valid wear adherence should be made during the study design phase so that wear instructions and engagement strategies are in place during the data collection and before the final analysis takes place. This view can be used during the collection stage to encourage participants to comply with wear adherence guidelines. In this way, long stretches of non-valid days in the processed data can be avoided.
