2.1 patients_summary.csv

Summary describing patient’s wear and activity from min_date to max_date. Following the steps described in Methods, daytime wear minutes are labeled and classified as adherent time or non-adherent time. Summary stats are computed across all the patient’s adherent wear time AWT or non-adherent wear NAW, column names make the distinction.

Column name Description
1 id Patient id.
2 min_date Patient’s first day in the study (or data export).
3 max_date Patient’s last day in the study (or data export).
4 daytime_hours Total number of daytime hours (between 8am and 8pm) in the study.
5 wear_daytime_hours Total number of daytime wear minutes (minutes with non-missing HR and non-missing step) aggregated in hours.
6 adherent_wear_hours Total number of daytime minutes that are adherent aggregated in hours.
7 nonadh_wear_hours Total number of daytime minutes that are non-adherent aggregated in hours.
8 daytime_wear_pct Proportion of weartime in the study during daytime.
9 nonadherent_ratio Ratio of total non-adherent over adherent time.
10 min_HR__<AWT, NAW> Patient’s minimum HR per minute value (average) within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
11 max_HR__<AWT, NAW> Patient’s maximum HR per minute value (average) within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW) minutes.
12 mean_HR__<AWT, NAW> Patient’s mean HR per minute value (average) within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW) minutes.
13 max_pos_steps__<AWT, NAW> Patient’s maximum number of steps per minute (count) within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
14 mean_pos_steps__<AWT, NAW> Patient’s average number of steps per minute (count) within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
15 zero_steps_prop__<AWT, NAW> Proportion of minutes with zero steps within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
16 sedentary_prop__<AWT, NAW> Proportion of sedentary minutes within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
17 light_prop__<AWT, NAW> Proportion of light activity minutes within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
18 moderate_prop__<AWT, NAW> Proportion of moderate activity minutes within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).
19 active_prop__<AWT, NAW> Proportion of active activity minutes within the adherent weartime (AWT) or non-adherent weartime (NAW).

2.2 daily_data.csv

This summary contains per-day values across adherent days.

Column name Description
1 id Patient’s id.
2 date Date.
3 index Number of days since first day in study (or since first date in the data export).
4 adherent_hours Total number of adherent minutes in `date` aggregated to hours.
5 mean_HR Average HR of adherent weartime minutes in `date`.
6 sd_HR HR’s standard dev of adherent weartime minutes in `date`.
7 zero_step_prop Proportion of adherent weartime minutes in `date` with zero steps.
8 sedentary_prop Proportion of adherent weartime minutes in `date` with sedentary activity.
9 light_prop Proportion of adherent weartime minutes in `date` with light activity.
10 moderate_prop Proportion of adherent weartime minutes in `date` with moderate activity.
11 active_prop Proportion of adherent weartime minutes in `date` with active activity.

2.3 daily_summary.csv

Patients’ summary statistics of daily physical activity in adherent days.

Column name Description
1 id Patient’s id.
2 mu_sedentary_prop Daily average (sample mean estimate) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in sedentary activity.
3 mu_light_prop Daily average (sample mean estimate) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in light activity.
4 mu_moderate_prop Daily average (sample mean estimate) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in moderate activity.
5 mu_active_prop Daily average (sample mean estimate) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in active activity.
6 sd_error_sedentary_prop Standard error (of the sample mean) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in sedentary activity.
7 sd_error_light_prop Standard error (of the sample mean) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in light activity.
8 sd_error_moderate_prop Standard error (of the sample mean) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in moderate activity.
9 sd_error_active_prop Standard error (of the sample mean) of the proportion of adherent weartime spent in active activity.
